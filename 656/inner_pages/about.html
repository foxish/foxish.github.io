Motivation

My motivation for taking up this course is the desire to study the evolution of media through history and the role it has played in shaping human lives. I believe this is key to understanding the ways in which new media changes the way we think. An understanding of new media can help in building new ways to present ideas and knowledge effectively, leading to faster growth & innovation.
